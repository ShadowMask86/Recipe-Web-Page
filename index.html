Misal Pav

⏱ 30 mins 🍽 Serves 4 🌶 Spicy

Ingredients

  • 2 cups sprouted moth beans (matki)
  • 1 onion (chopped)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1 tsp mustard seeds
  • 1 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tbsp red chili powder
  • 1 tbsp goda masala
  • Salt to taste
  • Farsan for topping
  • Pav (bread rolls)

Instructions

  1. Cook sprouted beans until soft.
  2. Heat oil and add mustard seeds.
  3. Add onions and sauté until golden.
  4. Add tomatoes and cook until soft.
  5. Mix turmeric, chili powder, goda masala, and salt.
  6. Add cooked beans and simmer for 10–15 minutes.
  7. Serve hot topped with farsan and pav.
